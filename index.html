About Celebal Technologies Project

Celebal Technologies is at the forefront of digital transformation, delivering AI and Big Data solutions to enterprises across industries. This project showcases a step-wise onboarding form designed to enhance user experience and streamline data collection processes.

With this stepper interface, Celebal aims to simplify user journeys, reduce form fatigue, and ensure seamless integration with backend data pipelines. Each step is crafted to collect essential information in an intuitive and visually engaging manner, aligning with Celebal's mission of combining innovation with simplicity.

The design is fully responsive, mobile-friendly, and accessible, keeping in line with modern UI/UX standards while offering customization options for various use cases like recruitment, client onboarding, or workflow automation.
